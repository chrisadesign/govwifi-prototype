Support

Individual problems

If an individual user is having trouble connecting to GovWifi:

  • direct them to our guidance on common issues
  • search our logs to confirm they are reaching our service
  • check theGovWifi service status

Organisation problems

If your entire organisation is having trouble connecting to GovWifi:

  • check your IP addresses and RADIUS secret keys match your local configuration
  • search our logs to confirm traffic from you is reaching our service
  • search our technical documentation
  • check theGovWifi service status

Request support

If you cannot resolve your issue, send us a support request.
